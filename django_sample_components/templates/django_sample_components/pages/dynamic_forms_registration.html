How It Works
  • Username sends a GET to the check endpoint on every keyup (400ms debounce, only when >3 chars) and swaps the feedback div inline
  • Subject sends a GET to the check endpoint on every change event, showing available spots or a full warning
  • Password and Date of Birth are validated only on full form submit
  • On valid submit, a success message replaces the form inside the component wrapper (hx-swap="outerHTML")
  • On invalid submit, the full component re-renders with crispy-forms error display
  • Validation data is mocked server-side — no database or migrations required
